Current Work

Below are a sample of experimental and philosophical projects I'm currently chipping away at.

  • Prosody and Reanalysis in Garden-Path Sentences: I investigate the relationship between prosodic phrase boundaries, garden-path sentence reanalysis, and lingering misinterpretations.
  • Language and Vocal Pitch Imitation: I examine how phonetic information in acoustic signals impacts speech/song imitation and perception.
  • Entity Realism and Mental Representations: I make the case that mental representations ought to be conceived of as theoretical posits rather than entities to be realist about.
  • Static Misinterpretations: I argue the causal properties of misinterpretations, arising from garden-paths, are best understood in light of static dispositions, i.e., a capacity to not change.
